CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

Welcome!

You have agreed to play our guessing game Crystal Collector.

This game is similar to a word guessing game; however, you will be guessing Numbers instead of letters!

Directions:

- Four crystals will display as buttons on the page near the bottom.

- You, the player will be given/shown a random number at the official start of the game.

- When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Heads Up!

You win if your total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game!!

However, you loose if your score goes above the original random number...sorry.
